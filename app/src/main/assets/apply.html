In order to be able to apply for courses, you will have to fill out a Participant application form. Filling out this form does not mean that you are automatically enrolled at MIND. This is just another method of applying to the institution.

You will be provided with a username and password and it is important that you retain your username and password as you will need them to apply for future courses without having to re-enter this information. Once received and processed, you will be contacted by a representative of MIND.

You can apply online via the website, APPLY HERE, for programmes currently being offered.
